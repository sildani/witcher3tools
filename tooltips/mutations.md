## ⚔️ Combat Mutations (Red)

### 1. Deadly Counter

* **Description:** Sword attacks deal **25%** more damage to human enemies immune to counterattacks and monsters. If an opponent is under **25% Vitality**, a successful counterattack immediately triggers a finishers animation, killing them instantly.
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Red Mutagens, 2 Ability Points

### 2. Bloodbath

* **Description:** Every successful melee strike with your sword increases your attack power by **5%** until the end of the combat encounter. This bonus stacks up to a maximum of **250%**. However, taking any damage from an enemy completely resets this bonus to 0%.
* **Requirements to Research:** 3 Greater Red Mutagens, 3 Ability Points

---

## 💨 Signs Mutations (Blue)

### 1. Magic Sensibility

* **Description:** Allows your offensive Signs to inflict critical hits. The critical hit chance scales directly with your overall Sign Intensity. Enemies killed by a Sign critical hit explode, dealing minor damage to nearby foes.
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Blue Mutagens, 2 Ability Points

### 2. Piercing Cold

* **Description:** Adds a freezing effect to the Aard Sign. Enemies caught in the blast have a **25%** chance to be instantly frozen (which kills them outright if they are simultaneously knocked down). Enemies who are not frozen take massive bonus frost damage instead.
* **Requirements to Research:** 3 Greater Blue Mutagens, 3 Ability Points

---

## 🧪 Alchemy Mutations (Green)

### 1. Toxic Blood

* **Description:** When an enemy hits you with a melee strike, they take immediate internal damage proportional to your current Potion Toxicity level. For every point of active Toxicity, the enemy takes **1.5%** of the damage they just dealt back as net damage.
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Green Mutagens, 2 Ability Points

### 2. Euphoria

* **Description:** For every point of active potion/decoction Toxicity you possess, your overall Sword Damage and Sign Intensity are increased by **0.75%**. *(Note: This caps out at a maximum bonus of **75%** in the Next-Gen version to curb infinite scaling).*
* **Requirements to Research:** 3 Greater Green Mutagens, 3 Ability Points

## 🔀 Hybrid Mutations (Dual-Color)

### 1. Conductors of Magic *(Combat + Signs)*

* **Description:** When you have a magic, unique, or Witcher sword drawn, the raw damage value displayed on that weapon is added directly to your base Sign damage calculations.
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Red Mutagens, 2 Greater Blue Mutagens, 3 Ability Points

### 2. Cat Eyes *(Combat + Alchemy)*

* **Description:** Crossbow damage is massively amplified. Crossbow bolts deal **700%** more raw damage and their baseline critical hit chance is raised by **50%**. Crossbow bolts also pierce through opponents, knocking them down or stunning them. If an enemy is at full health, a bolt instantly takes away **15%** of their Max Vitality.
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Red Mutagens, 2 Greater Green Mutagens, 3 Ability Points

### 3. Metamorphosis *(Signs + Alchemy)*

* **Description:** Landing a critical hit or applying a critical effect (like Burning or Bleeding) automatically activates a random Mutagen Decoction from your alchemy inventory for 120 seconds. This occurs without adding any Toxicity to your pool. Up to **3 decoctions** can be activated simultaneously this way.
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Blue Mutagens, 2 Greater Green Mutagens, 3 Ability Points

## 🌟 Advanced Mutations (Universal / All Colors)

*Note: Unlocking these pinnacle mutations allows their central slots to accept Combat (Red), Signs (Blue), OR Alchemy (Green) skills interchangeably.*

### 1. Mutated Skin *(Combat + Signs + Alchemy)*

* **Description:** Each available Adrenaline Point reduces all incoming damage taken by **15%** (up to a maximum cap of **45%** damage reduction at 3 Adrenaline Points).
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Red, 2 Greater Blue, 2 Greater Green Mutagens, 5 Ability Points

### 2. Adrenaline Rush *(Combat + Signs + Alchemy)*

* **Description:** Upon entering combat, your Attack Power and Sign Intensity are instantly increased by **30%** for each enemy present in the encounter for the first 30 seconds. However, after those 30 seconds pass, the buff drops off and your overall stats are reduced by **10%** per enemy for the remaining duration of the fight.
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Red, 2 Greater Blue, 2 Greater Green Mutagens, 5 Ability Points

### 3. Second Life *(Combat + Signs + Alchemy)*

* **Description:** When your Vitality is completely depleted to 0, Geralt becomes temporarily invulnerable, clears all negative status effects, and immediately restores **100% of his maximum Vitality**. This mutation has a fixed **180-second cooldown** between triggers.
* **Requirements to Research:** 2 Greater Red, 2 Greater Blue, 2 Greater Green Mutagens, 5 Ability Points
