💨 Aard Branch (Telekinetic Blast)

### 1. Far-Reaching Aard

* **Description:** Increases the radius of Aard's telekinetic blast.
* **Level 1:** Aard range increased by **1 meter**.
* **Level 2:** Aard range increased by **2 meters**.
* **Level 3:** Aard range increased by **3 meters**.

### 2. Aard Sweep *(Alternate Mode)*

* **Description:** **Hold** the Sign button to strike all opponents in a full 360-degree radius around Geralt.
* **Level 1:** Radially strikes enemies; knock-down chance is reduced by **21%**.
* **Level 2:** Radially strikes enemies; knock-down chance is reduced by **11%**.
* **Level 3:** Radially strikes enemies; knock-down chance is **no longer reduced**.

### 3. Aard Intensity

* **Description:** Directly increases the overall effectiveness and knock-down probability of Aard.
* **Level 1:** Aard Sign intensity increased by **10%**.
* **Level 2:** Aard Sign intensity increased by **20%**.
* **Level 3:** Aard Sign intensity increased by **30%**.

### 4. Shock Wave

* **Description:** Adds physical damage to the impact of the telekinetic blast.
* **Level 1:** Aard deals **100** physical damage.
* **Level 2:** Aard deals **200** physical damage.
* **Level 3:** Aard deals **300** physical damage.

## 🔥 Igni Branch (Fire Wave)

### 1. Melt Armor

* **Description:** Damage dealt by Igni permanently weakens enemy armor, leaving them vulnerable to physical sword attacks.
* **Level 1:** Reduces enemy armor by **15%** (scales with Sign intensity up to a maximum cap).
* **Level 2:** Reduces enemy armor by **30%**.
* **Level 3:** Reduces enemy armor by **45%**.

### 2. Firestream *(Alternate Mode)*

* **Description:** **Hold** the Sign button to emit a continuous, concentrated stream of flame that targets single enemies and forces shield-wielders to drop their guard.
* **Level 1:** Continuous stream unlocked; drains Stamina while active.
* **Level 2:** Continuous stream unlocked; slightly reduces Stamina drainage.
* **Level 3:** Continuous stream unlocked; drastically reduces Stamina drainage.

### 3. Igni Intensity

* **Description:** Directly boosts Igni's raw damage output and its percentage chance to trigger the continuous Burning status effect.
* **Level 1:** Igni Sign intensity increased by **10%**.
* **Level 2:** Igni Sign intensity increased by **20%**.
* **Level 3:** Igni Sign intensity increased by **30%**.

### 4. Pyromaniac

* **Description:** Significantly increases the base probability of setting enemies on fire.
* **Level 1:** Increases chance to apply Burning status by **20%**.
* **Level 2:** Increases chance to apply Burning status by **40%**.
* **Level 3:** Increases chance to apply Burning status by **60%**.

## 🔮 Yrden Branch (Magic Trap)

### 1. Sustained Glyphs

* **Description:** Extends the active life of Yrden traps and increases total trap capacity.
* **Level 1:** Adds **5 seconds** to Sign duration. Geralt can place 2 standard traps or increase alternate mode charges to 5.
* **Level 2:** Adds **10 seconds** to Sign duration. Geralt can place 2 standard traps or increase alternate mode charges to 7.
* **Level 3:** Adds **15 seconds** to Sign duration. Geralt can place 2 standard traps or increase alternate mode charges to 9.

### 2. Magic Trap *(Alternate Mode)*

* **Description:** **Hold** the Sign button to place an aggressive automated turret trap. It damages and slows all enemies within a 10-meter radius, while vaporizing incoming enemy projectiles (like arrows).
* **Level 1:** Alternate trap unlocked; deals minor damage.
* **Level 2:** Damage increased by **25%**; projectile destruction speed increased.
* **Level 3:** Damage increased by **50%**; projectile destruction speed maxed out.

### 3. Yrden Intensity

* **Description:** Strengthens the magnitude of the movement/attack slow effect and enhances the power of the alternate trap mode.
* **Level 1:** Yrden Sign intensity increased by **10%**.
* **Level 2:** Yrden Sign intensity increased by **20%**.
* **Level 3:** Yrden Sign intensity increased by **30%**.

### 4. Supercharged Glyphs

* **Description:** Enemies trapped inside the active Yrden matrix take passive damage over time, scaling directly against their maximum health pool.
* **Level 1:** Drains a minor percentage of enemy Max Vitality/Essence per second.
* **Level 2:** Drains a moderate percentage of enemy Max Vitality/Essence per second.
* **Level 3:** Drains a significant percentage of enemy Max Vitality/Essence per second (highly effective against bosses).

## 🛡️ Quen Branch (Protective Shield)

### 1. Exploding Shield

* **Description:** When your active Quen shield is completely depleted or broken by damage, it erupts outwards to stagger nearby threats.
* **Level 1:** Pushes enemies back with a small chance of knocking them down.
* **Level 2:** Pushes enemies back with a moderate chance of knockdown.
* **Level 3:** Pushes enemies back with a high chance of knockdown; inflicts minor physical damage.

### 2. Active Shield *(Alternate Mode)*

* **Description:** **Hold** the Sign button to form a continuous, spherical energy barrier. Absorbing incoming attacks no longer deals damage to you; instead, it consumes Stamina and heals Geralt.
* **Level 1:** Active sphere unlocked; maintaining it slowly drains Stamina.
* **Level 2:** Stamina drained by blocked attacks is reduced by **25%**.
* **Level 3:** Stamina drained by blocked attacks is reduced by **50%**.

### 3. Quen Intensity

* **Description:** Enhances the maximum amount of structural damage the shield can absorb before shattering.
* **Level 1:** Quen Sign intensity increased by **10%**.
* **Level 2:** Quen Sign intensity increased by **20%**.
* **Level 3:** Quen Sign intensity increased by **30%**.

### 4. Quen Discharge

* **Description:** Reflects a direct portion of the damage absorbed by standard Quen right back at the enemy who dealt it.
* **Level 1:** Reflects **10%** of absorbed damage back to the attacker.
* **Level 2:** Reflects **20%** of absorbed damage back to the attacker.
* **Level 3:** Reflects **30%** of absorbed damage back to the attacker.

## 🧠 Axii Branch (Mental Influence)

### 1. Delusion

* **Description:** Targets do not move toward Geralt while he is casting Axii. It also opens up unique dialogue opportunities in conversations, bypassing potential fights.
* **Level 1:** Target movement is slowed during casting; unlocks level 1 dialogue options.
* **Level 2:** Target movement is heavily slowed during casting; unlocks level 2 dialogue options.
* **Level 3:** Target movement is completely frozen during casting; unlocks level 3 dialogue options.

### 2. Puppet *(Alternate Mode)*

* **Description:** **Hold** the Sign button to turn an enemy into a temporary ally. They will immediately fight alongside you and deal amplified damage.
* **Level 1:** Target becomes an ally; deals **20%** more damage.
* **Level 2:** Target becomes an ally; deals **40%** more damage.
* **Level 3:** Target becomes an ally; deals **60%** more damage.

### 3. Axii Intensity

* **Description:** Increases the raw duration of Axii's stun effect.
* **Level 1:** Axii Sign intensity increased by **10%**.
* **Level 2:** Axii Sign intensity increased by **20%**.
* **Level 3:** Axii Sign intensity increased by **30%**.

### 4. Domination

* **Description:** Expands Axii’s cognitive influence, allowing you to manipulate multiple targets at once.
* **Level 1:** Axii can influence **2** targets simultaneously, though the stun effect's duration is reduced by **33%**.
* **Level 2:** Axii can influence **2** targets simultaneously, and the stun effect's duration is only reduced by **15%**.
* **Level 3:** Axii can influence **2** targets simultaneously with **no penalty** to the stun duration.
