## 🛡️ Armor Proficiency Skills

### Cat School Techniques

* **Description:** Each piece of Light Armor equipped increases critical hit damage by **25%** and fast attack damage by **5%**.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Griffin School Techniques

* **Description:** Each piece of Medium Armor equipped increases Sign Intensity by **5%** and Stamina regeneration in combat by **5%**.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Bear School Techniques

* **Description:** Each piece of Heavy Armor equipped increases strong attack damage by **5%** and maximum health (Vitality) by **5%**.
* **Max Level:** 1

## ⚡ Adrenaline & Combat Utility

### Focus

* **Description:** Adrenaline Points increase your weapon damage (as normal) but now also increase your **Sign damage** proportionally.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Adrenaline Burst

* **Description:** Increases global Adrenaline Point generation by **5%**. Furthermore, successfully casting Signs now also generates Adrenaline Points.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Rage Management

* **Description:** If your current Stamina pool is too low to cast a Sign, you can cast it by consuming **1 Adrenaline Point** instead.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Battle Frenzy

* **Description:** Replaces Battle Trance's default behavior: instead of increasing weapon damage, each active Adrenaline Point increases your **critical hit chance by 8%** (up to a maximum +24%).
* **Max Level:** 1

### Attack is the Best Defense

* **Description:** Generates Adrenaline Points when performing defensive maneuvers: Parries generate **0.1**, Counterattacks generate **0.4**, Dodges generate **0.3**, and Rolls generate **0.2** Adrenaline Points. (Has a 5-second internal cooldown).
* **Max Level:** 1

## 🧪 Toxicity & Resource Buffs

### Metabolism Control

* **Description:** Permanently increases your baseline maximum Toxicity pool by **30 points**.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Metabolism Boost

* **Description:** Consumes available Adrenaline Points to reduce the Toxicity cost of drinking potions by **33% per point** spent. (Does not apply to Mutagen Decoctions).
* **Max Level:** 1

## 🍞 Exploration & Survival Utility

### Gourmet

* **Description:** Consuming food triggers a Vitality regeneration effect that lasts for **20 continuous minutes** (rather than expiring after a few seconds).
* **Max Level:** 1

### Sun and Stars

* **Description:** During the daytime, Vitality regenerates by an extra **10 points per second** when out of combat. At nighttime, Stamina regenerates by an extra **1 point per second** during combat.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Survival Instinct

* **Description:** Increases your overall maximum Vitality by a flat **500 points**.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Strong Back

* **Description:** Permanently increases your maximum inventory carrying capacity limit by **60 points**.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Gorged on Power

* **Description:** The unique stat buff acquired from drawing from a Place of Power lasts **indefinitely**, rather than on a timer. However, you may only have one Place of Power buff active at any given time.
* **Max Level:** 1

## 🏹 Crossbow & Bomb Specialists

### Steady Shot

* **Description:** Crossbow bolts deal **25%** more raw damage.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Trick Shot

* **Description:** Allows Geralt to load and fire **one extra crossbow bolt** before being forced to play the reload animation.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Heavy Artillery

* **Description:** All offensive bomb damage is increased by a massive **200%**, but the maximum ammunition capacity of bombs in your equipment slots is cut in half (**50% reduction**). Does not affect status-only bombs.
* **Max Level:** 1

### Advanced Pyrotechnics

* **Description:** Direct damage dealt by any of your detonating bombs generates **0.1 Adrenaline Points**.
* **Max Level:** 1

### In Combat's Fires

* **Description:** Geralt becomes completely immune to the negative status side-effects of his own dropped bombs and specialty trick crossbow bolts.
* **Max Level:** 1
