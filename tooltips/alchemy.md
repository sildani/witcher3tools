## 🧪 Brewing Branch (Potions)

### 1. Heightened Tolerance

* **Description:** Increases the Potion Overdose threshold, allowing you to drink more standard potions before taking toxicity damage.
* **Level 1:** Overdose threshold increased to **60%**.
* **Level 2:** Overdose threshold increased to **70%**.
* **Level 3:** Overdose threshold increased to **80%**.

### 2. Refreshment

* **Description:** Imbibing any potion dose instantly heals a portion of your maximum Vitality.
* **Level 1:** Instantly heals **10%** of maximum Vitality.
* **Level 2:** Instantly heals **20%** of maximum Vitality.
* **Level 3:** Instantly heals **30%** of maximum Vitality.

### 3. Delayed Recovery

* **Description:** Potion effects do not wear off as long as your overall potion Toxicity remains high enough.
* **Level 1:** Potion effects don't wear off if Toxicity is above **85%** of maximum.
* **Level 2:** Potion effects don't wear off if Toxicity is above **70%** of maximum.
* **Level 3:** Potion effects don't wear off if Toxicity is above **55%** of maximum.

### 4. Side Effects

* **Description:** Drinking a potion grants a high chance of activating the effects of another randomly selected potion completely for free, without any extra Toxicity cost.
* **Level 1:** **33%** chance to activate an additional potion effect.
* **Level 2:** **66%** chance to activate an additional potion effect.
* **Level 3:** **100%** chance to activate an additional potion effect.

## ⚔️ Oil Preparation Branch

### 1. Poisoned Blades

* **Description:** Blade oils applied to your swords grant a baseline chance to poison the target on every hit. The effect scales with higher-tier oils.
* **Level 1:** **10%** poison chance.
* **Level 2:** **20%** poison chance.
* **Level 3:** **30%** poison chance.

### 2. Protective Coating

* **Description:** Grants protective damage resistance against attacks from the specific monster type targeted by the currently applied oil.
* **Level 1:** Adds **+5%** damage protection.
* **Level 2:** Adds **+10%** damage protection.
* **Level 3:** Adds **+15%** damage protection.

### 3. Fixative

* **Description:** Blade oils now have a significantly higher number of charges before wearing off.
* **Level 1:** Blade oils have **33%** more charges.
* **Level 2:** Blade oils have **66%** more charges.
* **Level 3:** Blade oils **never wear off** (infinite charges).

### 4. Hunter Instinct

* **Description:** When Adrenaline Points are at their maximum, critical hit damage against the targeted enemy type is increased.
* **Level 1:** Critical hit damage bonus increased by **33%**.
* **Level 2:** Critical hit damage bonus increased by **67%**.
* **Level 3:** Critical hit damage bonus increased by **100%**.

## 💣 Bomb Creation Branch

### 1. Steady Aim

* **Description:** Time is slowed significantly further while aiming a handheld bomb.
* **Level 1:** Time slowdown increased by **100%**. *(Note: Caps at Level 1)*

### 2. Pyrotechnics

* **Description:** Bombs that do not usually inflict physical damage (such as Samum or Devil's Puffball) now deal damage in addition to their normal debuff effects.
* **Level 1:** Bombs deal **50** bonus damage.
* **Level 2:** Bombs deal **100** bonus damage.
* **Level 3:** Bombs deal **150** bonus damage.

### 3. Efficiency

* **Description:** Increases the maximum carrying capacity of bombs in each inventory slot.
* **Level 1:** Maximum number of bombs in each slot increased by **1**.
* **Level 2:** Maximum number of bombs in each slot increased by **2**.
* **Level 3:** Maximum number of bombs in each slot increased by **3**.

### 4. Cluster Bombs

* **Description:** Upon initial detonation, bombs split apart into multiple explosive fragments.
* **Level 1:** Bomb separates into **2** explosive fragments.
* **Level 2:** Bomb separates into **4** explosive fragments.
* **Level 3:** Bomb separates into **6** explosive fragments.

## 🧪 Mutation Branch (Toxicity & Mutagens)

### 1. Acquired Tolerance

* **Description:** Expands your maximum Toxicity pool based on the number of alchemy recipes you have learned across the world.
* **Level 1:** Every known level 1, 2, or 3 alchemy recipe increases maximum Toxicity by **0.5 points**.
* **Level 2:** Every known level 1, 2, or 3 alchemy recipe increases maximum Toxicity by **1 point**.
* **Level 3:** Every known level 1, 2, or 3 alchemy recipe increases maximum Toxicity by **1.5 points**.

### 2. Tissue Transmutation

* **Description:** When a Mutagen Decoction is consumed, it temporarily boosts your maximum Vitality for the entire duration of the brew.
* **Level 1:** Adds **+300** maximum Vitality per active decoction.
* **Level 2:** Adds **+600** maximum Vitality per active decoction.
* **Level 3:** Adds **+900** maximum Vitality per active decoction.

### 3. Synergy

* **Description:** Boosts the unique stat bonuses provided by the Mutagens placed in your Character character slots.
* **Level 1:** Mutagen slot bonuses increased by **15%**.
* **Level 2:** Mutagen slot bonuses increased by **30%**.
* **Level 3:** Mutagen slot bonuses increased by **50%**.

### 4. Adaptability

* **Description:** Extends the total active duration of all Mutagen Decoctions.
* **Level 1:** Decoction duration increased by **10%**.
* **Level 2:** Decoction duration increased by **20%**.
* **Level 3:** Decoction duration increased by **30%**.

## 🩸 Trial of the Grasses Branch (Combat Toxicity)

### 1. Frenzy

* **Description:** If your potion Toxicity is above 0, automatic time slowdown is triggered whenever an enemy is about to perform a counterattack.
* **Level 1:** Automatically slows time when an enemy counters. *(Note: Caps at Level 1)*

### 2. Endure Pain

* **Description:** Increases maximum Vitality when overall Toxicity exceeds the safe threshold level.
* **Level 1:** Maximum Vitality increased by **10%**.
* **Level 2:** Maximum Vitality increased by **20%**.
* **Level 3:** Maximum Vitality increased by **30%**.

### 3. Fast Metabolism

* **Description:** Toxicity drops at a much faster rate per second, clearing out potion side effects rapidly.
* **Level 1:** Toxicity drops **1 point** faster per second.
* **Level 2:** Toxicity drops **2 points** faster per second.
* **Level 3:** Toxicity drops **3 points** faster per second.

### 4. Killing Spree

* **Description:** If potion Toxicity is above 0, each enemy killed drastically increases your chance to land a critical hit.
* **Level 1:** Critical hit chance increased by **15%** per kill.
* **Level 2:** Critical hit chance increased by **30%** per kill.
* **Level 3:** Critical hit chance increased by **50%** per kill.
