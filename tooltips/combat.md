# The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Next-Gen Combat Skill Tree

## ⚔️ Fast Attack Branch

### 1. Muscle Memory
* **Description:** Increases fast attack damage.
* **Level 1:** Fast attack damage +10%; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Fast attack damage +20%; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Fast attack damage +30%; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 2. Precise Blows
* **Description:** Increases critical hit chance and critical hit damage of fast attacks.
* **Level 1:** Critical hit chance +4%, Critical hit damage +25%; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Critical hit chance +8%, Critical hit damage +50%; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Critical hit chance +12%, Critical hit damage +75%; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 3. Whirl
* **Description:** A continuous spinning attack that strikes all foes in a surrounding area. Maintaining it consumes Stamina and Adrenaline.
* **Level 1:** Unlocks the Whirl ability; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Stamina and Adrenaline consumption reduced by **33%**; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Stamina and Adrenaline consumption reduced by **50%**; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 4. Crippling Strikes
* **Description:** Fast attacks apply a Bleeding effect that drains enemy Vitality or Essence. Duration: 5 seconds.
* **Level 1:** Bleeding deals **50** damage per second; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Bleeding deals **100** damage per second; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Bleeding deals **150** damage per second; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

## 🔨 Strong Attack Branch

### 1. Strength Training
* **Description:** Increases strong attack damage.
* **Level 1:** Strong attack damage +10%; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Strong attack damage +20%; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Strong attack damage +30%; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 2. Crushing Blows
* **Description:** Increases critical hit chance and critical hit damage of strong attacks.
* **Level 1:** Critical hit chance +4%, Critical hit damage +25%; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Critical hit chance +8%, Critical hit damage +50%; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Critical hit chance +12%, Critical hit damage +75%; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 3. Rend
* **Description:** A heavy overhead strike that deals massive damage proportional to stamina consumed and bypasses enemy defense. Adrenaline Points increase total damage.
* **Level 1:** Unlocks the Rend ability (increases critical hit chance by 20%); Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Critical hit chance bonus increased to 40%; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Critical hit chance bonus increased to 60%; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 4. Sunder Armor
* **Description:** Reduces the target's damage resistance when struck by a strong attack.
* **Level 1:** Reduces enemy damage resistance by **10%**; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Reduces enemy damage resistance by **20%**; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Reduces enemy damage resistance by **30%**; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

## 🛡️ Defense Branch

### 1. Arrow Deflection
* **Description:** Allows you to deflect incoming projectiles while parrying.
* **Level 1:** Deflect arrows while parrying; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Perfectly timed parries reflect arrows back at the attacker; Adrenaline Point gain +2%

### 2. Fleet-Footed
* **Description:** Grants complete protection from hits received while dodging.
* **Level 1:** Damage from hits received while dodging is reduced by **100%**; Adrenaline Point gain +1%

### 3. Counterattack
* **Description:** After a successful counterattack, your next attack deals significantly higher bonus damage.
* **Level 1:** Next attack deals **+30%** damage; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Next attack deals **+60%** damage, with a chance to knock down; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Next attack deals **+90%** damage, with an increased knockdown chance; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 4. Deadly Precision
* **Description:** Grants a chance to instantly kill an enemy on a successful hit if you possess Adrenaline Points.
* **Level 1:** Each Adrenaline Point adds a **1%** chance of an instant kill; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Each Adrenaline Point adds a **2%** chance of an instant kill; Adrenaline Point gain +2%

## 🏹 Marksmanship Branch

### 1. Lightning Reflexes
* **Description:** Time is slowed significantly further while aiming the crossbow.
* **Level 1:** Slows time by an additional **15%**; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Slows time by an additional **30%**; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Slows time by an additional **45%**; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 2. Cold Blood
* **Description:** Successfully hitting a target with a crossbow bolt generates instant Adrenaline Points.
* **Level 1:** Crossbow hits generate **0.1** Adrenaline Points; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Crossbow hits generate **0.2** Adrenaline Points; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Crossbow hits generate **0.3** Adrenaline Points; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 3. Anatomical Knowledge
* **Description:** Substantially increases crossbow critical hit chance.
* **Level 1:** Crossbow critical hit chance +20%; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Crossbow critical hit chance +40%; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Crossbow critical hit chance +60%; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 4. Crippling Shot
* **Description:** Critical hits dealt with the crossbow temporarily disable monster special abilities.
* **Level 1:** Disables monster abilities for **10 seconds**; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Disables monster abilities for **20 seconds**; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Disables monster abilities for **30 seconds**; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

## ⚡ Battle Trance Branch

### 1. Resolve
* **Description:** Minimizes or eliminates the loss of Adrenaline Points when taking damage.
* **Level 1:** Adrenaline Point loss upon taking damage is reduced by **33%**; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Adrenaline Point loss upon taking damage is reduced by **67%**; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Adrenaline Point loss upon taking damage is reduced by **100%** (No Adrenaline lost on hit); Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 2. Undying
* **Description:** When Vitality reaches 0, available Adrenaline Points are consumed to instantly restore health.
* **Level 1:** Restores **+33%** Vitality upon consumption; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Restores **+67%** Vitality upon consumption; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Restores **+100%** Vitality upon consumption; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 3. Razor Focus
* **Description:** Instantly grants an Adrenaline Point when entering combat, and increases Adrenaline generated from sword blows.
* **Level 1:** Instantly gain **1** Adrenaline Point when entering combat. Sword hits generate **5%** more Adrenaline; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Instantly gain **1** Adrenaline Point when entering combat. Sword hits generate **10%** more Adrenaline; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Instantly gain **1** Adrenaline Point when entering combat. Sword hits generate **15%** more Adrenaline; Adrenaline Point gain +3%

### 4. Flood of Anger
* **Description:** When casting a Sign, if 3 Adrenaline Points are available, they are consumed to upgrade the Sign to its highest tier (unlocking all bonuses) and massive Sign intensity.
* **Level 1:** Sign intensity increased by **100%**; Adrenaline Point gain +1%
* **Level 2:** Sign intensity increased by **200%**; Adrenaline Point gain +2%
* **Level 3:** Sign intensity increased by **300%**; Adrenaline Point gain +3%
